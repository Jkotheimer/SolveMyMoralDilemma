This program uses a utilitarianistic theory of morality to help you decide your moral dilemma.
There are hundreds of other theories to go off of that will give you many different answers,
but for the sake of simplicity, this program sums up the positivity rating (ultimate well-being)
that each outcome produces, and tells you to do the thing that produces the most well-being.
